## Inline formatting

**bold** — use `**text**`

*italic* — use `*text*`

***bold italic*** — use `***text***`

~~strikethrough~~ — use `~~text~~`

Inline `code` — use backticks

## Blockquotes

> Multi-line blockquotes work too.
> Just keep prefixing lines with `>`.
> — Author Name
